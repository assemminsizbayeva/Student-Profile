About Me

I'm Assem Minsizbayeva, a passionate Computer Science sophomore who strives to embody the values of service and leadership. As the current UCASA President and an active member of several student clubs, I'm deeply involved in student life and governance.

I founded a Service-Learning project in Naryn that supports local students in mathematics and English. With the involvement of 21 UCA volunteers, we have taught 91 schoolchildren in the 2024-2025 academic year.

I've also contributed to expanding this initiative to Razzakov (Batken region), working closely with local entrepreneur Ulanbek Myrzaev. Through this project, I aim not only to share knowledge but also to serve my community by empowering youth.
